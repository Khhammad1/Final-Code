ExporTech Futures

2 Years We Research For How To Growth Cotton Expertise & Farming Systems

The introduction of AI-based disease detection tools for cotton crops marks a significant advancement in modern agriculture. These tools leverage computer vision and machine learning to identify early signs of diseases such as bacterial blight, wilt, or leaf curl virus directly from images of the plants. Farmers can use a mobile app or a drone-based system to capture real-time data, which is then analyzed using trained AI models.

This allows for immediate identification of issues and timely intervention, helping to reduce crop loss and improve yield quality.
