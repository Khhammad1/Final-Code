01 Project Concept & Goals

This university project was initiated to provide innovative solutions for cotton farmers. Our goal was to create a web-based platform that uses Artificial Intelligence to detect crop diseases through image uploads, and simultaneously offer an export bidding system to connect local farmers with international buyers.

02 Designing the Solution

We designed an intuitive interface for farmers to upload crop images and receive AI-powered diagnosis. Alongside, we developed the structure of a real-time bidding module to ensure transparent and profitable export opportunities for cotton produce.

03 Development & Training

Our team gathered thousands of cotton crop images to train the AI model to recognize diseases such as wilt, boll rot, and leaf spots. Meanwhile, the export system was connected with buyer-side interfaces for seamless trade execution.

04 Final Implementation

The platform is now capable of diagnosing crop diseases instantly via image analysis and offering live export bidding functionality. This empowers farmers with early treatment suggestions and profitable selling options, directly from their fields to global markets.
