Who We Are?

AI-Powered Cotton Crop & Export Platform

We are a pioneering initiative focusing on the future of agriculture with a core mission: enhancing cotton crop health through Artificial Intelligence, and enabling direct export opportunities through a smart bidding system. Our platform helps farmers detect crop diseases early by simply uploading an image, and connects them to global buyers through a transparent and competitive export bidding marketplace.

  • AI-Powered Disease Detection by Image Upload
  • Real-Time Export Bidding System
  • Empowering Cotton Farmers with Smart Technology

Company History

Let’s Know About Our Project Journey

In 2020
Initiation of University Research

The concept was born as a final-year university project to address critical issues in the cotton industry using AI and digital innovation.

In 2021
AI Model Development

Using real-world datasets, we trained machine learning models to detect cotton diseases like leaf spots and boll rot from crop images.

In 2022
Web Platform Launch

We launched a user-friendly web platform that allows farmers to upload crop images for instant AI-based disease analysis and recommendations.

In 2023
Export Bidding Integration

We introduced a transparent export bidding feature, connecting local cotton producers directly with global buyers for fair trade opportunities.

In 2024
User Testing & Farmer Feedback

We collaborated with real farmers and export partners to test, refine, and validate the platform's performance and user experience in the field.

In 2025
Scaling for Impact

The project is now expanding across multiple cotton-growing regions, helping transform agriculture through AI and smart export systems.
